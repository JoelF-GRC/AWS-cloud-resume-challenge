About

A joule is unit of work or energy. This website is a collection of my work- paintings, drawing, and digital media.

Born in Naples, Italy, I spent my formative years on the East Coast of the United States. I received my BFA in Painting and Drawing from the University of the Arts in Philadelphia. Currently live in San Diego California.
